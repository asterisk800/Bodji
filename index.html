About Bodji

Bodji Schools alumni Association is established as a non- profit organization to bring together former students of Bodjii Dermedji and Bodjii Cheqorsa Schools (hereafter, Bodjii Schools) in order to get united for the common purposes of extending helping hands to these schools in Oromya Region of Ethiopia and be motivated in contributing financial, material, know-hows s well as resources towards promoting and strengthening the educational, social and economic development activities in Bodjii Schools and the surrounding communities.

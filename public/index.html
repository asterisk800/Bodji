Our Purpose

Bodji Schools Alumni Association (BSAA) is a non-profit organization established to bring together former students of Bodji Dirmedji and Bodji Cheqorsa schools located in Oromia Regional State of Ethiopia. The purpose of the organization is to support the schools and the surrounding communities professionally, financially, materially, as well as providing resources towards promoting and strengthening the educational, social, and economic developmental activities.
